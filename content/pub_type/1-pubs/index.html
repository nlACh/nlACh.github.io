    2025

    This work investigates the material-dependent charge qubit performance of a gate voltage-induced double-quantum-dot gate nanowire channel field-effect transistor (DQD-NWFET) device through charge stability, Bloch sphere coverage, anti-crossing energy, and dephasing time.
